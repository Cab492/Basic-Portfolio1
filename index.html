About Me

My name is Christian Barthlow. I am 23 years old and I live in Dallas, TX. I graduated from The University of Texas at Austin in May of 2017. I received a BA in Linguistics. After graduating I did not know what kind of career I wanted to enter. I spent some time studying to take the Texas Teacher's Exam. After a few months of preparing to be a teacher, I decided that it wasn't for me. I did some research and tried to think of ways to use my Linguistics degree to build a career. That is how I found Southern Methodist University's Coding Boot Camp. I started the program on October 25th, 2017 and I will graduate on April 28th, 2018! By the end of the program, I will become a full-stack developer.

I have a wide range of interests and ideas for linking the tech world to my linguistic knowledge and hobbies. Some of my interst include:

  • Natural Language Processing (NLP)
  • American Sign Language
  • Web Applications
  • Mobile Applications
  • Animals
  • Tennis
  • Pop Culture
  • Music
  • Religion
